"Creed" is a 2015 sports drama film that follows Adonis Johnson, the son of legendary boxer Apollo Creed. Determined to forge his own path, Adonis seeks training from Rocky Balboa and enters the boxing world. Overcoming challenges, he rises through the ranks and faces reigning champion Ricky Conlan. While Adonis doesn't win the fight, his performance solidifies his status as a formidable boxer and showcases themes of personal growth, redemption, and the pursuit of dreams.
