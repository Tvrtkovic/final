Creed 1 Creed 2 Creed 3 Forum What is Creed?

The "Creed" series is a collection of sports drama films that serve as a continuation and spin-off of the iconic "Rocky" franchise. The films center around the character of Adonis Johnson, the son of legendary boxer Apollo Creed. Adonis, played by Michael B. Jordan, follows his passion for boxing, seeking to establish his own legacy while honoring his father's name.
