The story picks up with Adonis Creed as he faces a new challenge in Viktor Drago, the son of Ivan Drago, the man responsible for Apollo Creed's death in the ring. Determined to avenge his father's legacy, Adonis trains harder than ever with Rocky Balboa by his side. The film explores themes of legacy, family, and forgiveness as Adonis confronts his past and steps into the ring to prove himself against an opponent with a dark history.
