In 2002 Los Angeles, a young Adonis "Donnie" Creed sneaks out with his best friend, Golden Gloves champion Damian "Diamond Dame" Anderson leading to a underground boxing mach. In the present day, Donnie has retired from boxing to focus on his wife Bianca and their daughter Amara, Released from prison, Dame reconnects with Donnie and shares his desire to resume his boxing career. Mary-Anne suffers another stroke and passes away. After her funeral, Donnie confesses to Bianca about the night of Dame's arrest, revealing Leon to be the abusive caregiver in their group home before he was adopted. Encouraged by Bianca, Donnie decides to come out of retirement and challenges Dame for the championship, which he accepts. Donnie faces Dame in the "Battle of Los Angeles" at Dodger Stadium. This leads in Adonis reclaiming the title and continuing the be the heavyweight champion.
